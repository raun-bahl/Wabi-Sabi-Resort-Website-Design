STUDIO

The Studio at Wabi Sabi is here to cater to all of your creative and spiritual needs. From pottery to traditional Japanese meditation techniques, experienced instructors are here to cater to you and your soul.

Arrive

Arrive according to your convenience at all times. Our studios are open 24x7, with our instructors and experts offering help from 9am to 5pm daily. Don’t forget to leave your stress at the door!

Unwind

Relax and cool down once in the studio. Help yourself to complimentary drinks and snacks at the counter, start a conversation with other kind souls, and prepare for some calm pottery.

Create

Focus on channelising your inner feelings onto the wheel. Shape, carve, chisel, and create anything that you would want, unleashing your inner creative. Group activities also available.

Relax

Take breaks, breathe and revitalize your soul. Work on different activities everyday, or focus on one core activity for the entirety of your stay. Cherish the art you made.

Overlooking the Seto Inland Sea, this tranquil space will help you unwind, relax and rejuvenate. The studio is a space for you to create and cherish pottery and japanese visual art. Opening up to a verdant forest from the backside, take a break and go for a lush, peaceful walk. Book a spot now!
